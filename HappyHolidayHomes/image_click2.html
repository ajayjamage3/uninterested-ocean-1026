Coorg Homestays

Coorg is known for the hospitality of people and beautiful scenery. This is one of the best destinations for nature lovers and romantics in India. Tourists get fascinated by colourful scenery, wooded slopes, undulating landscape and quaint villages of Coorg. A short and scenic drive from Bangalore, Coorg is best experienced by staying in a Coffee Plantation homestay. Important places to visit while you are there include Madikeri Fort, Raja's Seat, Bagamandala temple, Dubare Elephant Camp and Iruppu Falls. If you have a little extra time, try to also visit Nagarhole National Park. Tripvillas has the best collection of Homestays in Coorg - most of them have been personally inspected by our teams.
